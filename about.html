About TVN Connect

Welcome to TVN Connect

TVN Connect is an unofficial online platform created exclusively for the members of Tulsi Vidya Niketan (TVN) School. Our platform is designed to keep the community alive and thriving, preserving school memories while providing a space for communication.

What You Can Do on TVN Connect

  • Share Memories: Upload photos, text posts, and relive moments together.
  • Stay Connected: Chat with fellow students and teachers in a private, secure space.
  • Access Prebuilt Letters: Ready-made formats for leave, half-day, and more.
  • Shop: Browse and shop for school merchandise and products.
  • Exclusive Features: More exciting features to keep the TVN spirit alive!

Our Mission

TVN Connect was built by INERT to provide TVN school members with a platform where they can share memories, stay connected, and access useful resources. We aim to preserve the friendships and experiences that define our time at TVN.

About INERT

INERT specializes in creating customized projects, apps, websites, and platforms related to connectivity, communication, and social media. Our mission is to build solutions that help people stay connected in the digital world.

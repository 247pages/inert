About the App

TVN Connect is an unofficial platform for Tulsi Vidya Niketan School members, designed to preserve school memories and maintain connections. Users can share photos, chat securely, access prebuilt letters, and shop for school merchandise. Created by INERT, the platform aims to keep the TVN community connected and thriving.
